ZMAPP

ZMapp is an experimental drug developed by Mapp Biopharmaceutical. ZMappTM is composed of three “humanized” monoclonal antibodies manufactured in plants, specifically Nicotiana [tobacco]. It is an optimized cocktail combining the best components of MB-003 (Mapp) and ZMAb (Defyrus/PHAC). It is manufactured at Kentucky BioProcessing in Owensboro. Production of the drug takes several weeks and only produces a small amount. Current side effects of the drug include chest tightness and increased heart rate. More side effects will become known with continuous observations.
